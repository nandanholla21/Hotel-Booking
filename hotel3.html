Where you will be

Margao,Goa,India

Quinta Da Santana is a 15 min drive from Margao, The commercial city and shopping centre of South Goa. Dabolim airport- 25 kms Railway station- 15kms The nearest town is Fatorda where there are supermarkets, Banks, Shops and Restaurants which is 5 kms away. The Manor house shares its neighbourhood with the likes of Rachol Seminary and Ancient Churches. The neighbouring village of Curtorim and Loutolim are also beautiful and scenic adorned with rivers, lakes and fields. A walk around the sleepy village of Raia is highly recommended.

Getting around


The ideal way of getting around from Quinta Da Santana is to hire a two wheeler or a four wheeler which are provided by us to our guests at a very low cost. The Cars available are a Maruti 800 non A/c, Maruti Suzuki Alto and Hundai i10. One can also hire taxi’s which are made available for a tour of North Goa or South Goa or to visit any of nearby beaches. Public transport is also available to Margao city. Guests who travel solo can opt for the local motorcycle taxi (locally called pilots), which are very economical, as an alternative to taxis. If you wish to indulge in delicious Goan food, there are a several restaurants in the vicinity like Nostalgia, Aura and Anushka’s to name a few. Food can also be ordered via their home delivery service.
