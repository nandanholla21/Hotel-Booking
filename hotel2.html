What you will see


When in Puducherry whether you’re trying to see the sights or splurge some money there is something for everyone. Our personal favorites when it comes to attractions are Rock beach, the French War Memorial and Bharathi Park. We also highly recommend taking a day trip to Auroville which is about 13 kilometers away. There you will find a very interesting town and the main attraction, Auroville’s Golden Globe which in itself is a sight to behold. When it comes to shopping and dining we suggest visiting the Sunday market if you are here for the weekend. Mission street is also a prime location in the shopping district and is a host to a variety of stores. Promenade beach contains many shops as well if you’re looking for handmade items or souvenirs. In terms of dining there are all types of cuisine in Puducherry and none will disappoint. Our favorites are Le Cafe which is on Promenade Beach, Asian House, Cafe Xtasi and Baker’s Street (good for breakfast). All of these restaurants will give you an amazing cultural experience while also allowing you to explore the culinary expertise of Puducherry. Transportation is easily accessible in Puducherry. You will see autos everywhere or if you prefer taking the wheel you can access many bike rental shops located nearby on Mission street. But because you will be so close to everything walking is definitely an option as well!

Getting around


All methods of transportation are easily accessible when staying at Le Tranquil. The area is packed with autos which can be called at the end of the street (They don't come through the street until called as to avoid noise pollution). If you prefer taking the wheel yourself you can go down to mission street 2km away which has a variety of Motorcycle, car and even bicycle rentals.
